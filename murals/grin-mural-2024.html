Grin Mural, 2024

Large-scale “Grin” mural created by Ron English in Greenpoint, Brooklyn, as part of the Public Pool community art initiative.

Work details

Title
Grin
Year
2024
Location
Greenpoint, Brooklyn, New York, United States
Context
Created as part of Public Pool, a community art initiative

Description

Ron English created this large-scale Grin mural in early 2024 as part of Public Pool in Greenpoint. The work merges his grinning skull imagery with the classic yellow smiley, a visual motif he has reworked across murals, prints, and paintings for decades. Painted on a vivid red exterior wall, the mural quickly became a prominent neighborhood landmark and was widely photographed during and after completion.
