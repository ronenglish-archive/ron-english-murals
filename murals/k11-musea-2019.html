K11 MUSEA, 2019

Permanent installation at the Opera Theatre in K11 MUSEA, Hong Kong, featuring Ron English’s Ronnie Rabbits and related POPaganda imagery.

Work details

Title
Ronnie Rabbits Delusion
Year
2019
Location
Opera Theatre, K11 MUSEA, Hong Kong
Program
K11 MUSEA Permanent Art Collection

Description

Commissioned for Hong Kong’s K11 MUSEA during the Victoria Dockside redevelopment, this panoramic auditorium installation features Ron English’s Ronnie Rabbits and other POPaganda iconography across an expansive wall inside the Opera Theatre. The project forms part of K11 MUSEA’s permanent art collection and reflects the institution’s effort to merge contemporary art, architecture, and public-facing cultural space.
