Fashion Moda, 1988

Temporary façade mural painted by Ron English for his 1988 exhibition at Fashion Moda in the South Bronx.

Work details

Title
Fashion Moda façade mural
Year
1988
Location
Fashion Moda, South Bronx, New York, United States
Context
Created for Ron English’s 1988 exhibition at Fashion Moda

Description

For his 1988 exhibition at Fashion Moda, Ron English painted the art space’s entire street-facing façade with a sequence of heart forms and hybrid figures, covering the storefront with temporary panels. According to English’s later recollections, the installation survived only about a week before the whole painted façade was removed overnight, turning the piece into a short-lived episode in Fashion Moda’s history of experimental, street-visible projects.
