Large-scale mural in Lyon, France, featuring Ron English’s hybrid-animal POPaganda imagery.

Description

Ron English’s Elefanka brought his hybrid-animal surrealism into Lyon’s growing landscape of monumental street murals. Local guides and walking-tour coverage identify the wall as one of the city’s notable large-format urban artworks, placing English’s POPaganda imagery within Lyon’s expanding public-art scene.
