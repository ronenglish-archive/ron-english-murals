Plessy School, New Orleans, 2022

Community mural created by Ron English with local students at the historic Plessy School in New Orleans.

Work details

Title
Plessy School mural
Year
2022 (May 27)
Location
Plessy School, New Orleans, Louisiana, United States
Program
Community mural / school collaboration project

Description

Commissioned as part of a community-engagement initiative at the historic Plessy School, this mural brought Ron English together with local students to design and paint a large public artwork. The project merges English’s POPaganda imagery with neighborhood participation, extending his public-art practice into a collaborative civic setting rooted in education and place.
