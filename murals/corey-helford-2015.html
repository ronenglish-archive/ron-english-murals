Corey Helford, 2015

Outdoor mural painted by Ron English at Corey Helford Gallery in Los Angeles, featuring the artist’s Monarch Elephant imagery.

Work details

Title
Monarch Elephant
Year
2015 (December 8)
Location
Corey Helford Gallery, 571 S. Anderson Street, Los Angeles, California, United States
Context
Painted on the exterior of Corey Helford Gallery’s new Arts District space

Description

On December 8, 2015, Ron English painted a new outdoor mural on the side of the newly opened Corey Helford Gallery in the Los Angeles Arts District. The work features an orange Monarch Elephant, combining elephant imagery with butterfly-wing patterning in a form that became closely associated with English’s public work from this period.

Installed at the gallery’s new 12,000-square-foot location on South Anderson Street, the mural quickly became a recognizable landmark for the site and remains one of English’s most widely photographed Los Angeles wall works.
