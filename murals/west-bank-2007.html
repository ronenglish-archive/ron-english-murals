West Bank Barrier Mural, 2007

Protest mural intervention by Ron English on the West Bank separation barrier near Bethlehem, combining political satire with anti-oppression imagery.

Work details

Title
Pardon Our Oppression
Year
2007
Location
Bethlehem area, Palestine — West Bank separation barrier
Type
Protest mural intervention

Description

In 2007, Ron English contributed a striking intervention to the West Bank separation barrier near Bethlehem. The wall had become an open-air protest site for international street artists, and English’s work stood out for its combination of painted sections, poster-like treatments, and direct political messaging.

The mural combines fragmented red-and-white fields with a militarized clown figure and the stenciled phrase Pardon Our Oppression. The imagery reflects English’s long-running critique of propaganda, militarism, and the visual language of power.
