Manhattan Runners, 2017

Ron English’s NYC Marathon mural created along the TCS New York City Marathon route on Manhattan’s East Side.

Work details

Title
Manhattan Runners
Year
2017
Location
1st Avenue at St. Marks Place, Manhattan, New York, United States
Event
TCS New York City Marathon official mural program

Description

Painted along the route of the TCS New York City Marathon, Manhattan Runners turned a stretch of 1st Avenue into a temporary public-art landmark. The mural functioned as both street art and a live visual marker for thousands of runners and spectators moving through Manhattan during one of the city’s largest annual events.
