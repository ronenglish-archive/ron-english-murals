Wynwood Walls, 2018

Ron English’s contribution to Wynwood Walls during Miami Art Week 2018, part of the annual Basel-season programming in Miami.

Description

During Miami Art Week 2018, Wynwood Walls expanded its annual Basel-season programming with live painting sessions and a roster that combined established muralists with rising international artists. Ron English contributed new POPaganda imagery to the complex, helping anchor that year’s refreshed visual momentum across Wynwood.
