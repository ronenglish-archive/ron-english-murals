Urban Bigfoot, 2010

Large-scale mural created for the early LA Freewalls initiative in the Los Angeles Arts District.

Work details

Title
Urban Bigfoot
Year
2010
Location
Los Angeles Arts District, Los Angeles, California, United States
Context
Created as part of the early LA Freewalls initiative

Description

One of the most recognizable works from the early LA Freewalls initiative, Urban Bigfoot blended Ron English’s POPaganda surrealism with the raw energy of the emerging Arts District. The towering figure became a frequent subject for photographers and appeared regularly in discussions of Los Angeles’ shifting relationship to street art and public murals.
