ONE WALL, 2017

Mural created for Urban Nation’s inaugural ONE WALL program in Berlin, Germany, and added to the museum’s permanent outdoor collection.

Description

Created for Urban Nation’s opening-season ONE WALL program, this mural became part of the museum’s permanent outdoor collection in Berlin. Ron English’s contribution stood out within the launch lineup and aligned with Urban Nation’s broader mission to treat city façades as an open-air gallery, placing his work among a global roster of artists selected for the institution’s debut phase.
