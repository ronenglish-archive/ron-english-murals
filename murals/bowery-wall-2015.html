Bowery Wall, 2015

Ron English’s mural for the Bowery and Houston Wall in New York City, featuring Temper Tot against a stylized American flag.

Work details

Title
American Depopulation
Year
2015
Location
Bowery and Houston Wall, New York, New York, United States
Context
Commissioned mural for the landmark Bowery wall site

Description

Ron English’s takeover of the Bowery and Houston Wall featured his baby Hulk figure, Temper Tot, surging forward against a monumental American flag. Contemporary coverage framed the mural as a pointed commentary on consumer culture, political theater, and national spectacle. Installed on one of New York’s most closely watched mural sites, the work quickly became one of the most photographed Bowery wall projects of 2015.
