Reynosa Border Wall, 2011

Border-wall interventions by Ron English in Reynosa, Tamaulipas, including the works You Must Be This Color and Double Donkey.

Work details

Title
You Must Be This Color and Double Donkey
Year
2011
Location
Reynosa, Tamaulipas, Mexico — near the U.S.–Mexico border
Type
Border-wall interventions / political public works

Description

These interventions were created near the U.S.–Mexico border in Reynosa and belong to Ron English’s politically charged body of public work addressing nationalism, race, and border policy. The titles You Must Be This Color and Double Donkey frame the site as both a literal barrier and a space for satirical critique.

As with many of English’s public interventions, the works connect billboard activism, subvertising, and street-level political commentary. Academic and journalistic sources discussing the project place it within a broader history of border imagery and anti-authoritarian visual tactics in his work.
