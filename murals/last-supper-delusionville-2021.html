Last Supper in Delusionville, 2021

Large-scale mural created during Art Basel Miami 2021 in Wynwood, featuring Ron English’s Delusionville characters in a parody of Leonardo da Vinci’s Last Supper.

Work details

Title

Year

Context
Created during Art Basel Miami 2021 and Wynwood Art Week activity

Description

During Art Basel Miami 2021, Ron English created a monumental mural titled The Last Supper in Delusionville, staging an ensemble of his stylized animal characters in a satirical reworking of Leonardo da Vinci’s Last Supper. Painted in Wynwood during the annual flood of street-art activations, the mural became one of the most photographed Ron English works from that year.
