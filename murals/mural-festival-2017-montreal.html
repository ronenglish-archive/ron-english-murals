MURAL Festival, Montreal, 2017

Ron English’s Sourire Mona Lisa, created for the 2017 MURAL Festival in Montréal and installed along Boulevard Saint-Laurent.

Work details

Title
Sourire Mona Lisa
Year
2017
Location
Boulevard Saint-Laurent, Montréal, Quebec, Canada
Context
Created for the 2017 edition of MURAL Festival

Description

Ron English headlined the 2017 MURAL Festival with Sourire Mona Lisa, a large outdoor work that blends classical portraiture with POPaganda surrealism. Installed along Boulevard Saint-Laurent, the mural became one of the most photographed works of that year’s festival and was highlighted in both Canadian arts coverage and international street-art reporting.
