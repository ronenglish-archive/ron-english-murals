Beacon PopMart, 2018

Mural created for Ron English’s Beacon POP MART pop-up in Beacon, New York, featuring the character Montana Mouse.

Work details

Title
Montana Mouse on the House
Year
2018
Location
45 Beekman Street, Beacon, New York, United States
Context
Created for the Beacon POP MART pop-up shop

Description

Painted for Ron English’s Beacon POP MART pop-up, this mural features a neon, oversized Montana Mouse spraying paint toward the viewer. The work draws on English’s established pop-surrealist imagery and his larger Delusionville universe, translating those themes into a public-facing wall tied to the temporary Beacon project.
