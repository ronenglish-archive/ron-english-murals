Miami PopMart, 2019

Entrance mural created for Ron English’s POP MART Delusionville pop-up during Art Basel Miami 2019 in Wynwood.

Work details

Title
Entrance mural for POP MART Delusionville
Year
2019
Location
Wynwood, Miami, Florida, United States
Event
POP MART Delusionville pop-up during Art Basel Miami 2019

Description

Created for Ron English’s POP MART Delusionville pop-up in Wynwood during Art Basel Miami 2019, this entrance mural introduced visitors to the installation with the artist’s signature POPaganda imagery. Painted as part of the larger Delusionville environment, the work functioned as both a street-facing landmark and a visual threshold into the pop-up experience.
