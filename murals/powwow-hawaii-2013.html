POW! WOW! Hawaii, 2013

Ron English’s underwater-themed mural created for POW! WOW! Hawaii 2013 in Honolulu.

Work details

Title
Underwater Poker Game
Year
2013
Location
Kakaʻako district, Honolulu, Hawaii, United States
Event
POW! WOW! Hawaii

Description

For the 2013 edition of POW! WOW! Hawaii, Ron English painted a large-scale ocean-themed mural featuring sharks, whales, dolphins, and an octopus gathered around a poker table. The playful underwater tableau became one of the most recognizable murals of that year’s festival and appears widely in festival documentation and street-art photo archives.
