Zelephant, South Haven, 2014

Ron English’s Zelephant mural, painted live during the Waterfront Film Festival in South Haven, Michigan.

Description

Ron English painted the Zelephant mural during the Waterfront Film Festival in South Haven, Michigan, in June 2014. The festival invited artists to create live outdoor works, and English contributed this hybrid zebra-elephant figure, a recurring character in his POPaganda universe. Painted in a single day, the bright red-and-yellow wall quickly became one of the most photographed pieces of the 2014 event.
