Jersey City, Cole Street, 2009

Collaborative mural painted in Jersey City by Ron English, Bigfoot, and Jason Maloney in 2009.

Work details

Title
Cole Street mural
Year
2009 (March)
Location
Hudson County Art Supply building, Cole Street / First Street, Jersey City, New Jersey, United States
Collaborating artists
Ron English, Bigfoot, Jason Maloney

Description

In March 2009, Ron English, Bigfoot, and Jason Maloney painted a collaborative mural on the exterior of Hudson County Art Supply in downtown Jersey City, at the corner of Cole Street and First Street. The project formed part of an early push to bring large-scale public art into the city and was noted in contemporary coverage as an effort to turn neglected urban surfaces into visible community landmarks.
