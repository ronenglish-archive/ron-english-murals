RXSM / Spider House Ballroom, 2015

Massive exterior mural created in Austin by Ron English and Kelly “RISK” Graval during the 6th annual RXSM Self-Medicated Film Expo.

Description

Painted during the 6th annual RXSM Self-Medicated Film Expo in the middle of SXSW, this large exterior wall became a high-visibility collaboration between Ron English and Kelly “RISK” Graval. The mural replaced Spider House’s long-standing movie-themed wall with a saturated POPaganda composition built from skull-smiley imagery, explosive color, and a provocative cow figure that drew attention in Austin press.

Coverage from Popaganda, Montana Cans, and local media framed the project as one of the defining mural moments of that festival season, bringing Ron English’s visual language into direct dialogue with RISK’s chromatic spray-paint style.
