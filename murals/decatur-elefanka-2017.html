Decatur Elefanka, 2017

Ron English’s Elefanka mural in Decatur, Illinois, painted on the exterior wall of Ken’s Aquarium & Pet Supply.

Work details

Title
Elefanka
Year
2017
Location
Ken’s Aquarium & Pet Supply, 730 E. Cerro Gordo Street, Decatur, Illinois, United States
Context
Commissioned as part of the Decatur Area Arts Council’s Mural Project

Description

In 2017 Ron English returned to his hometown of Decatur to create Elefanka, a mural painted on the exterior wall of Ken’s Aquarium & Pet Supply. The character, part of English’s POPaganda universe, joined other public works commissioned through the Decatur Area Arts Council’s mural initiative and connected his long-running visual language to the city where he was born.
