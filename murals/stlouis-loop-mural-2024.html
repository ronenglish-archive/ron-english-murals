St. Louis Loop Mural, 2024

Mural created by Ron English for the 2024 Loop Mural Festival in the Delmar Loop district of St. Louis, Missouri.

Description

Ron English participated in the 2024 Loop Mural Festival in St. Louis, contributing a mural unveiled around May 31, 2024. Contemporary coverage framed the festival as part of a broader effort to revitalize the Delmar Loop through large-scale public artworks, and English’s participation was confirmed through both local news reporting and the artist’s own social-media post from the site.
