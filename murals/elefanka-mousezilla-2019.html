Elefanka and Mousezilla, 2019

Two-wall mural installation created for the Jersey City Mural Arts Program, pairing Ron English’s Elefanka and Mousezilla characters at Erie Street in Jersey City.

Work details

Title
Elefanka and Mousezilla
Year
2019
Location
Erie Street basketball court, 266 Erie Street, Jersey City, New Jersey, United States
Program
Jersey City Mural Arts Program

Description

Commissioned as part of the Jersey City Mural Arts Program, this two-wall, sixty-foot installation pairs a butterfly-winged orange Elefanka with a towering green Mousezilla, both drawn from Ron English’s Delusionville universe. Painted overlooking a neighborhood schoolyard, the mural became one of Jersey City’s signature POPaganda landmarks and a key example of English’s public work in the city.
