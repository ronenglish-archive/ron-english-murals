Texas Tantrum Trots and Pals, 2022

Large-scale mural created by Ron English in Deep Ellum, Dallas, blending POPaganda characters with Texas-themed surrealism.

Work details

Title
Texas Tantrum Trots and Pals
Year
2022 (November)
Location
Deep Ellum, Dallas, Texas, United States
Program
Deep Ellum neighborhood mural initiative

Description

Installed in the heart of Deep Ellum’s arts district, Texas Tantrum Trots and Pals marked Ron English’s return to Texas with one of the area’s most flamboyant recent murals. Blending POPaganda characters with Texas-themed surrealism, the work quickly became a local landmark within a district long associated with bold public art and visual experimentation.
